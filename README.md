# Global Shoe Sales Analysis

## Project Description
This project presents a top-level analysis of Global shoe sales dataset using Excel, Power Query, SQL and Power BI. This analysis helped uncover key business insights related to revenue generation, customer preferences, sales channels, product performance, and regional demand patterns. Additionally, Executive-level questions relating to strategic decision-making for retail growth, pricing optimization, product focus, and market expansion were explored.

## Tools & Technologies Used
- Excel – Data Cleaning & Transformation
- Power Query - Data Cleaning & Transformation 
- SQL – Queried the dataset to get KPIs and answers to the Business Questions
- Power BI – Data visualization & dashboard development

# Core Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Revenue Generated
- Total Units Sold
- Average Revenue per Sale
- Revenue by Country
- Revenue by Brand
- Revenue by Shoe Type
- Revenue by Sales Channel
- Quarterly Revenue Trend
- Units Sold by Price Category
- Average Order Value by Channel

# Business/Executive Level Questions
- Which brands contribute the most to total revenue?
- What time periods (months/quarters) generate the highest revenue, and why? / Are we seeing growth or decline trends in sales over time?
- Which countries/regions generate the highest sales and revenue?
- How does customer preference differ by country (e.g., shoe type, brand)? (USE COUNTRY AS SLICER)
- Which performs better: Online vs Mall sales in terms of revenue and volume?
- What channel shows the highest average order value?
- Which shoe types (Boots, Running, Casual, etc.) sell the most? / Which products generate the highest revenue vs highest volume (not always the same)?
- How does price affect units sold? Are higher-priced shoes actually more profitable, or do lower-priced ones sell more?
- Which colors are most popular, and do these vary by country?
- What is the average revenue per sale across channels and regions?

# Key Insights
1. UAE Generated the Highest Revenue: The UAE recorded the highest shoe purchase revenue among all analyzed countries, indicating a strong premium retail market and higher customer spending power.
2. High-Priced Shoes Generated Strong Revenue: Premium-priced shoes contributed significantly to total revenue, proving that customers are willing to spend more on high-value footwear products.
3. Sneakers Had the Highest Demand: Sneakers recorded the highest sales among all shoe categories, showing strong customer preference for casual and versatile footwear.
4. Skechers Contributed the Most Revenue: Among all brands analyzed, Skechers generated the highest overall revenue, outperforming competitors such as Nike, Adidas, and Puma.
5. Retail Stores Remain the Strongest Sales Channel: Retail stores generated slightly higher revenue compared to mall and online channels, suggesting customers still value physical shopping experiences for footwear purchases.
6. Revenue Peaked in the Fourth Quarter: The fourth quarter showed the highest revenue performance, likely influenced by holiday shopping trends and seasonal demand increases.

# Recommendations
1. Increase Investment in Sneaker Inventory: Since sneakers generated the highest demand, the company should prioritize sneaker-focused marketing campaigns and inventory expansion.
2. Expand Premium Product Marketing in High-Revenue Regions: Regions such as the UAE showed strong purchasing power for premium shoes. Hence, more premium collections and targeted promotions should be introduced in these markets.
3. Strengthen Skechers Partnership and Product Availability: Since Skechers generated the highest revenue, increasing stock availability and promotional visibility could further improve profitability.
4. Prepare Aggressively for Q4 Sales Peaks: The company should optimize inventory, staffing, and marketing ahead of Q4 to maximize seasonal revenue opportunities.
5. Improve Online Sales Experience: Although retail stores performed best, online channels remain competitive. Improving digital customer experience and delivery efficiency could increase online revenue contribution.
6. Optimize Pricing Strategy: Mid-priced shoes achieved high sales volume while premium shoes generated high revenue. A balanced pricing strategy should be maintained to capture both affordability-focused and premium customers.
